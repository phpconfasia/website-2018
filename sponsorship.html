Sponsorship

Thanks you for your interest in supporting the PHPConf.Asia 2018. The conference is scheduled for between 26 & 29 September 2018 in Singapore. 26 September will be a full-day PHPUnit Workshop conducted by the creator of PHPUnit, Sebastian Bergmann. Followed by 2 days of conference talks at NUSS Kent Ridge Guild House. The Tutorial Day will be on 29 September where developers will have a full day of hands on tutorials lead by international speakers.

This will be *the* Pan-Asian PHP conference - bringing together the PHP communities from Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia, Vietnam, the Philippines and many more.

Some highlights:

  • Up to 300 PHP developers & professionals from across Asia
  • 20 international & regional speakers
  • Registration for email alerts is available now: http://phpconf.asia
  • Call for proposals are open right now: https://cfp.phpconf.asia
  • Tickets are on sale now.

For become a sponsorship, please email admin@phpconf.asia
